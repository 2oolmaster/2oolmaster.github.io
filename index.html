Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

At 2oolmaster, we prioritize your data privacy above everything else. Because all our tools are compiled and executed entirely on the client-side (inside your web browser), **we do not collect, store, or transmit your personal inputs to any external server.**

1. Data Processing: Any information you enter (including names, resumes, passwords, and memos) remains local. Memos are saved safely within your browser's Local Storage cache.

2. Third-Party Services: Our QR Generator securely accesses a free public API strictly to compile your text strings into standard image layouts. No user profiles are created.

3. Cookies: We only save essential client preferences, specifically your dark/light UI theme selection.
